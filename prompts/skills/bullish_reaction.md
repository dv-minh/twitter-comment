# Skill: `bullish_reaction`

## Use for
Tweets driven by hype, conviction, momentum, or strong bullish sentiment.

## Goal
Match the energy fast and sharp, anchor to one real signal instead of pure vibe amplification.

## Reply instincts
- Anchor excitement to one catalyst, timing signal, positioning setup, or narrative strength.
- Amplify when the tweet references:
  - a clear catalyst
  - market structure
  - positioning
  - momentum
  - onchain/activity signals
- Skip heavy amplification if the tweet is pure emotion with no signal.
- Sound excited, not euphoric or manic.
- One clean punch is stronger than stacked hype phrases.
- Mild CT energy works better than forced slang.

If the reply depends mainly on reasoning, positioning, or market interpretation, it likely belongs to `market_analysis` instead.

## Good examples
- "timing on this narrative feels way too clean tbh"
- "market still feels underpositioned for this move"
- "this is where reflexivity starts kicking in"

## Bad examples
- "1000x incoming"
- "everyone will be rich"
- "ape now"
- "guaranteed send"

## Avoid
- guaranteed outcome language
- price targets
- stacked hype slang
- influencer/shill-bot energy
